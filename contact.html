Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer dairy-free milk alternatives?

Yes, we offer Oat, Almond, and Soy milk for all our espresso and tea-based drinks.

Are pets allowed inside?

We welcome well-behaved, leashed dogs on our outdoor patio area, but only service animals are permitted inside the cafe.

Do you have reliable WiFi for working?

Absolutely. We provide complimentary high-speed fiber internet for all our paying customers. Outlets are available near most seating areas.
